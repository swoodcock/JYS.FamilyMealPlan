Tater Tot Casserole
Saved from week of Jul 20–26, 2026 · serves 4 + lunchbox
▾
Allergen-safeIron-richHidden vegLow-carb swap
Allergen-safe: The classic uses peas — swap to corn + green beans. Cream soup & cheese bake in = cooked dairy (no raw dairy). No egg anywhere.

Ingredients

  • 700 g lean ground beef iron
  • 1 onion (~150 g), diced · 2 carrots (~150 g), grated · 1 zucchini (~150 g), grated hidden veg
  • 60 g baby spinach, finely chopped iron + fiber
  • 300 g can cream of mushroom soup or roux (15 g flour + 360 ml milk)
  • 150 g corn + 150 g green beans, chopped no peas
  • 15 g (1 tbsp) tomato paste vit C
  • 450 g frozen tater tots · 100 g shredded cheddar
  • Salt, pepper, ½ tsp garlic powder

Method

  1. Brown beef with onion. Stir in carrot, zucchini, spinach; cook 3–4 min.
  2. Add tomato paste, corn, green beans, soup (or roux). Season. Spread in a baking dish.
  3. Top with tater tots, then cheese. Bake 200°C (400°F) 30–35 min until golden.
🔄 Low-carb: tots are high-GI. Plate a portion from the beef-veg layer + a scoop of mashed cauliflower instead of tots.
🩸 Iron-rich: beef + spinach + tomato paste. Serve with orange segments to boost iron absorption.
🥡 Lunchbox: reheats perfectly — microwave 60–90 s.

Mexican Lasagna
Saved from week of Jul 20–26, 2026 · serves 4 + lunchbox
▾
Allergen-safeIron-rich ★Hidden vegLow-carb swapAdult spice
Allergen-safe: Skip raw sour cream on the allergen-safe portion. Melted cheese = fine. No egg/nuts. Black beans replace any peas.

Ingredients

  • 570 g ground beef (or half beef / half turkey) iron
  • 400 g can black beans, drained & rinsed iron + fiber ★
  • 1 onion (~150 g) + 1 bell pepper (~150 g), diced · 150 g corn hidden veg
  • 60 g baby spinach, chopped iron
  • 400 g can diced tomatoes + 240 g mild salsa vit C
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning (mild), 1 tsp cumin
  • 8 corn or whole-grain tortillas · 150 g shredded Mexican-blend cheese
  • 1 avocado (for the low-carb portion) · Adults: jalapeños / hot sauce · optional sour cream (not the allergen-safe portion)

Method

  1. Brown beef + onion. Add pepper, corn, spinach, beans, tomatoes, salsa, spices. Simmer 8 min.
  2. Layer: sauce → tortillas → sauce → cheese, repeat; finish with cheese.
  3. Bake 190°C (375°F) 25 min. Rest 5 min before cutting.
🔄 Low-carb: beans stay (low-GI); tortillas are the load. Build a tortilla-free skillet portion over lettuce, or 1 low-carb tortilla + avocado.
🌶️ Adults: stir jalapeños/hot sauce into one half before layering, or add on top after plating the plain pieces.
🩸 Iron-rich ★: the week's iron star — beef + black beans + spinach + salsa vitamin C.
🥡 Lunchbox: a firm square reheats cleanly; no sour cream.

Meatloaf + Mashed Potatoes
Thursday dinner → Friday lunch · serves 4 + lunchbox · Saved Jul 21, 2026
▾
Allergen-safeIron-richHidden vegLow-carb swapAdult spice
Allergen-safe: Standard meatloaf uses egg as binder → use a flax egg (10 g / 1 tbsp ground flax + 45 ml water, rest 5 min). Milk in the breadcrumbs cooks in (no raw dairy). No peas in the side.

Ingredients

  • 700 g ground beef iron
  • Binder: 80 g breadcrumbs (egg-free) + 80 ml milk + 1 flax egg
  • 1 grated carrot, ½ grated zucchini, 1 small onion, 60 g finely chopped spinach hidden veg + iron
  • Glaze: 60 g ketchup + 15 g tomato paste + splash Worcestershire
  • Mash: 900 g potatoes + butter + milk — plus 1 cauliflower head (~600 g, for the low-carb mash)
  • Side veg: carrots + 200 g green beans no peas
  • Adults: sriracha stirred into extra glaze

Method

  1. Mix beef, soaked breadcrumbs, flax egg, grated veg, spinach, salt, pepper. Press into a loaf pan.
  2. Brush with glaze. Bake 190°C (375°F) 45–55 min (internal 71°C / 160°F).
  3. Boil potatoes (and cauliflower separately for the low-carb mash); mash with butter + milk. Steam carrots + green beans.
🔄 Low-carb: swap mash for mashed cauliflower (or 50/50 blend). Make extra → it doubles as Sunday's tater-tot swap.
🩸 Iron-rich: beef + spinach hidden in the loaf. Serve with the tomato glaze (vit C) + green beans.
🌶️ Adults: brush your slices with sriracha-spiked glaze.
🥡 Lunchbox: a slice + a scoop of mash reheats great.
📝 Notes: A real family favorite - make extra glaze next time
